*Sections and subsections:

The WOA!! website is organized into sections: i.e. categories. Each section is accessed from the navigation bar on the home page.

An example of a section is Sustainability. Its subsections include: Energy, Water, Consumption, Agriculture, and so on.

Sections can occupy one or more pages. For example, Energy has its own page.

This admin screen is set up to allow section editors and administrators to move, view, and otherwise maintain groups of items by selecting a subsection, choosing some or all items within the subsection, and performing a function on them.
