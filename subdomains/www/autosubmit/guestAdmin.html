Instructions for
WOA!! Autosubmit Guest Administrator
  1. Changes: If changes to an article need to be made, follow the instructions below.

  2. Summarized: For many of the articles, you will need to go to Suggested/Summarized Articles and check for 'Summarized' articles. There should be a gray button at the end of the article. Click on that and follow the instructions below to proof-read, tidy-up, and advance the article to its next location - the News Digest (preliminary to putting it on the Public page). After you have processed all of the articles in 'Summarized', proceed to the next step.

  3. Preliminary review: Check out pages before sending them to the Public Page. Go to 'important links'. Look at the News Digest page and see if both top and bottom of page are there and no 'Transfer Interrupted' or anything else weird in the middle. If they are, simply refresh the link. The graphics will be missing - they don't generate on the fly. Also briefly look at each of the Headline Links below(a few seconds after each page comes up). If you get some strange message, try the link again. This will generate the page again and likely get rid of most of the problems.

  4. Advance - Click here (once only) to move the News Digest and Headlines pages to the public population-awareness and also FTP to overpopulation.org .(this really does move it to the public page, so don't click on it unless you are ready).. Look for the messages. The 'move' part goes fast, but the FTP takes awhile. Do not wait too long for FTP - sometimes your browser bogs down even though the files are sent - just proceed to step 5.

  5. Final review: Check the two sites below to make sure they FTP'd OK (use 'Refresh' or 'Reload' to make sure you have the most recent copy of the page) and make sure the date on the pages equals today's date (repeat the 'Advance' step above if the date is still not right after refreshing or reloading):

    How to Change an Article

    Put the following URL in your address box on your browser, change document ID in the last 6 digits of the URL, and with the cursor still in the address box after the URL, hit ENTER.
    http://www.population-awareness.net/cgi-bin/cgiwrap/popaware/article.pl?display%login%006490
    If you do not have the document number, go to one of the addresses above and look for the article by using search on the title or part of the title (hopefully someone will have told you where to look for the article).

    There should be a red arrow or a grey button, or something (sometimes invisible) to click on to the bottom right of (after the end of) the article. Click on it.

    This should bring you to a login box - enter your account number and click 'GO'.

    Then you should see the WOA Article/Item Submittal form. It is a large form with many options, but Publish Date, Source, Headline, Link, Summary, and Full Article are the important ones. Plus at the bottom are one or two columns that control where the article is to be placed. When using the 'Add item to 1 or more section_subsection(s):' scroll box, you may choose more than one option. If you choose more than one, hold down the CTRL key while you select the 2nd and more.

    However, to just move the article along to the public page, check the 'Advance this item' and skip the above-mentioned scroll box.

    When finished, hit SUBMIT.
